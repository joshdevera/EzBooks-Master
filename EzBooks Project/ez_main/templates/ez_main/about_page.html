About Simple Scholar

The goal of Simple Scholar is to provide a simple, and convenient method for you, as a busy student, to get your textbooks for the semester!

The Team

The Simple Scholar Team

Consisting of several college students who know all to well the experience of getting textbooks the hard way, the Simple Scholar team set out to solve these devastating stuggles for all future students.

Services

Services

Simple Scholar offers delivery of your textbook order directly to your dorm room. In addition, all orders are available for pickup at the MK Great Hall within 30 minutes of placing your order.
